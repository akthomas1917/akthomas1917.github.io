About Me

My name is Andrew Thomas and I'm a computer science major at Stony Brook University.
I've been programming for about four years now and mainly have experience with Java,
HTML, CSS, and some Python. In my classes, I mainly use object-oriented programming,
and outside of school, I've been teaching myself web development (which I used to code
this website), however my real interest is in cyber security (which I plan to specialize in my
junior year). As of now, I'm planning to pursue a Master's degree in computer science after
graduating from Stony Brook, but I'm
still considering other options as well.

Outside of classes, my two biggest passions are sports and music. I've played trumpet
and piano for about 10 years now, and currently play in the Spirit of Stony Brook
Marching Band. I'm also a huge hip-hop/rap fan - some of my favorite artists include
Logic, J. Cole, Kendrick Lamar, Childish Gambino, and many more. I like making my own
covers of my favorite songs using the skills I've learned in band (you can check out some
of my covers in the "Music" tab). I've also been a huge basketball fan from a young age
and love playing when I get the chance. My favorite team is the Celtics and my favorite
player is Kyrie Irving.
